ThreatLens does not collect, store, or transmit any personal user data.



\- Your AbuseIPDB API key is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API and is never sent anywhere except directly to AbuseIPDB when you trigger a scan.

\- IP addresses and domains are only sent to AbuseIPDB when you explicitly click the scan button.

\- No browsing history, page content, or usage data is collected or logged.
